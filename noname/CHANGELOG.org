* Revision history for noname

** 0.3 -- 2024-11-21

- Support for choosing between cvc5 and z3.
- Fix redundant analysis of key terms.

** 0.2 -- 2024-02-05

- Addition of Haddock comments for most of the code.
- Support for multiple cores.
- Rewriting of the parser (with megaparsec instead of the previously
  used alex+happy).

** 0.1.0.0 -- 2023-05-15

- Release of the first version of the tool, submitted to CSF 2024 as
  additional material for the paper
  [[https://doi.ieeecomputersociety.org/10.1109/CSF61375.2024.00011][/A
  Decision Procedure for Alpha-Beta Privacy for a Bounded Number of
  Transitions/]] by Fernet L., Mödersheim S. and Viganò L.
